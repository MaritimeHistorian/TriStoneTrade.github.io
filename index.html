Tri-Stone Trade Publications

Exploring trade in the Pacific world through history and lived experience

My name is Pierce McDonnell. Tri-Stone Trade Publications is a personal project centered on my current work with the Pacific Transportation Association, using it as a lens to engage with the evolving dynamics of trade. I am particularly interested in building stronger bridges across the Pacific world, among companies, academic institutions, and professional organizations.
