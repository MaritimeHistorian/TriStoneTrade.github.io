About

Pierce McDonnell & Tri-Stone Trade Publications

My name is Pierce McDonnell. Tri-Stone Trade Publications is a personal project centered on my current work with the Pacific Transportation Association, using it as a lens to engage with the evolving dynamics of trade. I am particularly interested in building stronger bridges across the Pacific world, among companies, institutions, and professional organizations.

Current Focus: The Pacific Transportation Association

This project is currently anchored in my involvement with the Pacific Transportation Association. Through research, events, and industry engagement, it serves as a platform to strengthen relationships across carriers, shippers, and logistics professionals operating in transpacific and broader Pacific networks.

At the same time, it provides a space to document and explore the Association's history and its ongoing role in shaping dialogue around trade, transportation, and policy.

The Name “Tri-Stone”

磊

“Tri-Stone” refers to my Chinese name, 磊 (Lěi), a character formed by three stones stacked together. It conveys a sense of candor and openness, as in the phrase 光明磊落 (guāng míng lěi luò, “clear and upright”). That spirit reflects how I approach this project: as an open effort to connect people and build lasting relationships across the Pacific.
